The Social Media Registry is a tool to keep track of social media accounts managed by federal agencies. It helps the public identify “official” federal social media accounts and helps agencies manage their social media efforts.

These widgets demonstrate the power of the Registry and the API—so anyone can access content from across thousands of federal social media accounts, updated in real-time.

Promote your public facing mobile websites and apps by registering it in the Federal Mobile Products Registry! Registration gets your app listed on USA.gov’s Federal Mobile Apps Directory, the DigitalGov platform and included in a government mobile products API.

The Federal Mobile Apps Registry feeds the Federal Mobile Products API, which is the authoritative source for federal mobile Web products (apps and mobile Web).

When you register data about your public-facing English and/or Spanish mobile websites and native apps, they are automatically included in a federal government-wide mobile products API, which feeds USA.gov’s Federal Mobile Apps Directory and Directorio Federal de Aplicaciones (Apps) Móviles page(s). Your mobile product will also be eligible to be featured in an article in the DigitalGov.gov mobile channel.
